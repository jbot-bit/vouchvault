# Terms of Service for Bots

## Overview

Telegram Bots are third-party applications operating within Telegram's ecosystem. This document governs user interactions with bots while using Telegram's Bot Feature (TBF).

## Key Sections

### 1. Acceptance of Bot Terms

Users must accept these terms plus Telegram's main Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. "Your continued access to and use of TBF shall constitute your acceptance of these Terms" and any modifications made over time.

### 2. Bots

Third-party Service Providers (SP) own and operate all bots independently from Telegram. Critical points include:

- Users access third-party services directly from SPs
- Telegram provides payment access only; it doesn't process transactions
- SPs control content quality, performance, and availability
- Users assume all transaction risks
- Refunds and cancellations follow individual SP policies

**Availability:** SPs may discontinue bots anytime. Telegram bears no liability for lost access to tokens, funds, data, or assets.

### 3. Payments

Telegram does not process payments or store credit card information. Third-party payment providers handle all transactions per SP selection.

**Disputes:** Payment issues must be directed to the payment provider or SP, not Telegram.

### 4. Privacy

**Data Sharing:** Bots can access user data as detailed in Telegram's Privacy Policy section 6.3. Users acknowledge understanding this policy.

**User-Provided Data:** Information shared directly with bots goes to the SP, who bears responsibility for handling it.

**Telegram Business:** Business account bots follow developer guidelines but remain third-party services. Users indemnify Telegram for unexpected bot actions.

### 5. Disclaimers

TBF is provided "as is" with no warranties. Telegram disclaims responsibility for:

- Service reliability, accuracy, or error-free operation
- SP-provided goods or services
- Price data errors or transmission delays
- Maintenance interruptions
- Third-party illegal actions
- User negligence or violations

**Legal Recourse:** All disputes should be directed exclusively toward relevant SPs.

**Indemnity:** Users indemnify Telegram against claims arising from their bot usage or SP transactions.

### 6. TBF Modification

Telegram may modify or discontinue features without notice or liability, including restricting access to specific bots or users.

### 7. Changes to Terms

Updates to these terms become effective when posted at [https://telegram.org/tos/bots](https://telegram.org/tos/bots).
